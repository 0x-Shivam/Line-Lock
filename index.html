Traffic Routing Survival

>> Objective

Guide colored dots from their hubs to matching gates by drawing roads. Survive.

!! Death

  • Two different colored dots collide
  • Any dot drifts off-screen

[] Controls

  • Click + Drag to draw a road
  • Release to place permanently
  • Mouse or touch works

--> Road Rules

  • Roads are one-way -- arrow shows direction
  • Roads are permanent -- plan ahead
  • Minimum length: 25px

i How It Works

Dots drift slowly toward their gate. Touch a road -- lock on and speed to the end, then resume drifting. Same-color dots are safe; different colors must stay apart.
